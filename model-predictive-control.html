Model Predictive Control

An Introduction

A comprehensive course covering the theory and practice of Model Predictive Control — from optimisation fundamentals and state-space modelling through constrained optimal control, Kalman filtering, and robust tube MPC.

Level: MSc / Advanced Undergraduate Chapters: 11 + Appendices Parts: Fundamentals · Optimal Control · MPC

From classical feedback control to optimisation-based control. The four key components of MPC: model, measurements, objectives, and constraints.
